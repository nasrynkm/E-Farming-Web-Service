    • Marketing Information

    Market information allows farmers to understand current and future demand trends, enabling them to align their production accordingly. By staying informed about market conditions, farmers can anticipate shifts in consumer preferences, identify emerging opportunities, and adjust their planting schedules or livestock production to meet the demand. This alignment helps prevent overproduction or shortages, ensuring a steady supply of desired products and maximizing profitability for farmers.

    It plays a vital role in price determination. By having access to current market prices, farmers can make informed decisions about pricing their products. They can evaluate supply and demand dynamics, monitor price fluctuations, and set competitive prices that are both fair to the customer and profitable for their business. Similarly, customers benefit from market information by being aware of prevailing prices, allowing them to make informed purchasing decisions.
